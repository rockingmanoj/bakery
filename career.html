Careers with Bakery

Patisserie Valerie has a long standing reputation for quality and service. Our staff are very important to us as we know that our team members are a key factor to this success.

We offer honest, hardworking and motivated people an opportunity to progress in an exciting, challenging working environment. Whether you work in the spotlight of personal customer service or behind the scenes in the kitchen or bakery areas we operate an in-house promotion policy where by all employees are encouraged to fulfil their potential.

We work hard to build great teams in all areas of our business and recruit people (with appropriate work permit or Home Office documentation) from all over the world.

Want to work for us?

We currently have around 2000 employees throughout the UK based in our stores, bakeries, transport, warehouses and head office. With expansions plans seeing more and more stores open nationwide, we are always on the lookout to expand our team.

Details of our current vacancies can be found in the menus to the left.
